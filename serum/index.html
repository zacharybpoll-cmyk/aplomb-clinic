APLOMB. The Serum

For the face in the mirror.

Seven active clinical ingredients, formulated for the specific skin pattern GLP-1 medications produce: volume loss underneath, elasticity change above, compressed timeline.

$129 30 mL · 60-day supply

What it is.

A water-based serum in a 30 mL frosted-glass bottle. One to two drops to clean skin, morning or evening, applied to mid-face, jawline, and neck. Sits cleanly under sunscreen, makeup, or any moisturizer you already use.

Built for women on a GLP-1 who are seeing the early signs of facial volume loss in the mirror, or who want to stay ahead of it. Works as well in month one as it does in month seven, but the earlier you start, the less you have to undo.

What it treats.

Ozempic face: the simultaneous fat-compartment volume loss and dermal-signaling change that GLP-1 medications drive.

Fifty to one hundred millilitres of facial fat-compartment volume disappears as the body draws down its stores. In parallel, GLP-1 receptor activation on dermal fibroblasts impairs collagen and elastin synthesis, independent of the weight loss itself. The pattern is distinct from age-related thinning, and it compresses into nine to sixteen weeks.

Why that matters.

What you see in the mirror at month four: a hollowed mid-face, deepened nasolabial folds, a jawline that has softened, the look of a face that has aged ten years in twelve weeks. Once the volume is gone, filler is the only intervention — an average $5,000 spend in a face that previously cost $1,000-$2,000 to maintain.

The Serum does not replace lost fat compartments. It supports the dermal layer above them, so what stays of the cushion underneath is supported by collagen, elastin, and antioxidant defence rather than thinning.

How it works.

Three peptides plus four plant actives, each chosen for a specific role in the pattern:

  • Matrixyl 3000
    Palmitoyl Tripeptide-1 + Palmitoyl Tetrapeptide-7. Stimulates fibroblast collagen and elastin synthesis — the pathway that thickens the dermis from underneath.
  • Matrixyl Synthe’6
    Palmitoyl Tripeptide-38. Stimulates six dermal-matrix proteins. Reduces wrinkle depth and smooths the surface above.
  • Centella Asiatica
    Triterpene saponins. Long-standing collagen-synthesis support; the same active in scar-care protocols.
  • Kakadu Plum
    The highest natural source of vitamin C. Antioxidant defence + cofactor for collagen synthesis.
  • Bearberry leaf extract
    Arbutin. Supports tone evenness over the same window the volume loss compresses into.
  • Licorice root
    Glycyrrhizin + glabridin. Anti-inflammatory and brightening, paired with bearberry for a luminous-not-glowing finish.
  • Hyaluronic acid (low-MW)
    The carrier that gets all of the above through the stratum corneum and into the layer where the fibroblasts are.
