# Aplomb Hair Thinning — Private-Label Supplier Alternates (v1)

**Objective:** Identify 3–5 white-label marine collagen + multi-deficiency capsule formulations that beat the Vox Nutrition "Hair, Skin & Nails Capsule" baseline (46/100 on 7-dimension rubric).

**Research Period:** May 2026  
**Scope:** GLP-1 telogen effluvium (TE) drivers addressed: caloric/protein insufficiency, sub-clinical iron deficiency, sub-clinical zinc deficiency, vitamin D/B12 deficiency.

---

## Clinical Foundation

**Milani et al. 2023** (N=83, 3-month double-blind RCT):  
Marine collagen peptides (10g/day) + iron + selenium + amino acids significantly improved:
- Hair density (phototrichogram-measured, p<0.05)
- Reduced telogen percentage (morphology shift, p<0.05)
- Efficacy in women with self-perceived thinning, AGA, FAGA, and telogen effluvium

**Supporting Evidence:**
- Patel 2017: Iron deficiency limits biotin absorption in TE patients
- Durusu Turkoglu 2024: Serum zinc 49.91 vs 60.53 µg/dL in TE patients (p=0.004)
- Marine collagen peptide bioavailability: 2000–5000 Daltons, Type I collagen, 90%+ absorption in 3 hours
