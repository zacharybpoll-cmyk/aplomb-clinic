What it is.

A 120-capsule bottle, 4 capsules daily, taken with food. Dosed for absorption, not just label numbers. Vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, third-party tested in a GMP-certified US facility.

Built to sit alongside a baseline lab panel at month one and a follow-up at month four. Daily is not a substitute for a prescriber-ordered iron infusion or a B12 shot if your bloodwork is already deep in the deficiency column — it is the maintenance and prevention layer underneath.

What it treats.

The nutrient deficiencies that GLP-1 medications drive through smaller meals, slower gastric emptying, and narrower food variety. Twenty-two percent of GLP-1 users hit a measurable deficiency by month twelve.

B12, iron, vitamin D, and calcium are the four that move first. Sixty to seventy percent of users fall below recommended intake for calcium alone. The downstream of those numbers is what shows up in the mirror as hair, skin, fatigue, and recovery.

Why that matters.

Bloodwork that nobody is checking unless you ask for it. Ferritin under 30 and B12 trending toward 200 are easy to miss until the hair starts shedding or the energy starts dropping in week eight. Once you see them downstream, you are already several months behind on replacement.

Daily is the prevention play: enough of each of the five most-likely-to-move nutrients to hold them at the population sufficiency line through the first twelve months on a GLP-1.

How it works.

Five active families, each in the form most consistently absorbed:

  • Methylcobalamin B12
    The active, methylated form. The form most consistently raised in the published replacement studies.
  • Ferrous bisglycinate (iron)
    Chelated iron. Gentler on the gut than ferrous sulfate; better absorbed at lower doses.
  • Vitamin D3 + K2
    Cholecalciferol with K2 (MK-7). K2 directs calcium where it belongs — bone, not artery.
  • Zinc picolinate
    The form with the strongest absorption data, dosed for skin and hair structure plus immune support.
  • Magnesium glycinate
    The chelated form. Better tolerated than oxide, and dosed for sleep, muscle recovery, and the mood layer underneath.
