What it treats.

The nausea wave that follows every dose escalation. GLP-1 medications slow gastric emptying — that is the whole point for blood sugar; it is also the source of the nausea. In the STEP-1 trial, 44% of semaglutide users reported nausea, peaking in weeks one to four of titration and at every dose increase.

It is the single largest reason people quit before they get to the dose that works.

Why that matters.

Nausea is the discontinuation driver in every published GLP-1 trial. The window is short — week one peaks, week three is hardest, week eight quiets if you titrate properly. The right intervention timed to that window is the difference between staying on long enough for the drug to work and quitting before it does.

Calm does not blunt the drug or change titration. It supports the GI side effect through the same evidence base used in the pregnancy and chemotherapy nausea literature, ported to the adjacent population on GLP-1.

How it works.

Three components, each at a dose with published evidence in adjacent populations:

  • Ginger root extract
    Standardised, dosed at 1 g/day. The Cochrane review covers 26 RCTs in pregnancy and chemotherapy nausea, with a 30-50% reduction at this dose.
  • Vitamin B6 (pyridoxine)
    The same B6 used in pregnancy nausea protocols. Adjacent population, same delayed-emptying mechanism. Adds when ginger plateaus.
  • Electrolytes (sodium, potassium, magnesium)
    Sugar-free stick packs. Smaller meals plus occasional vomiting moves you toward dehydration faster than you notice. ORS evidence base.

What we cite for nausea on GLP-1s.

RCT incidence + duration

  • Wilding et al., STEP-1 trial, 2021. 44% of users report nausea; median episode duration 8 days; severity worst in titration. NEJM.
  • Jastreboff et al., SURMOUNT-1, 2022. Phase III RCT of tirzepatide. Nausea 17-22% across doses. NEJM.

Adjacent-population evidence

  • Cochrane Review, 2014. Ginger for nausea and vomiting in pregnancy. 26 RCTs. 30-50% reduction at 1 g/day.
  • Matthews et al., 2015. Vitamin B6 for nausea in pregnancy. 30-60% reduction vs placebo.
