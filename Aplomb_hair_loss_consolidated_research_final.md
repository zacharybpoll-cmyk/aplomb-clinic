## Clinical Foundation (All Lanes)

### Systemic Multi-Deficiency Drivers (Lane B)
**Milani et al. 2023** (N=83, 3-month double-blind RCT):  
Marine collagen peptides (10g/day) + iron + selenium + amino acids significantly improved hair density (phototrichogram-measured, p<0.05) and reduced telogen percentage (morphology shift, p<0.05). Efficacy demonstrated in women with self-perceived thinning, AGA, FAGA, and telogen effluvium.

**Supporting Evidence:**
- **Patel 2017:** Iron deficiency limits biotin absorption in telogen effluvium patients (iron supplementation restores biotin bioavailability)
- **Durusu Turkoglu 2024:** Serum zinc 49.91 vs 60.53 µg/dL in TE patients (p=0.004); zinc deficiency correlates with telogen phase duration
- **Marine Collagen Peptide Bioavailability:** 2,000–5,000 Daltons (Type I), 90%+ absorption in 3 hours; therapeutic dose range 2,000–10,000mg/day

### Topical & Format-Shifter Drivers (Lane C)
**Panahi et al. 2015** (N=100, 6-month double-blind RCT):  
Rosemary oil (1% concentration, twice daily) demonstrated **non-inferiority to minoxidil 2%** in male androgenetic alopecia. Comparable hair regrowth (phototrichogram-measured) at 6 months.

**Peptide Growth Factor Evidence:**
- **sh-Polypeptide-1 (IGF-1 mimic):** Hair follicle proliferation via insulin-like growth factor pathway
- **sh-Polypeptide-9 (KGF):** Keratinocyte growth factor, extends anagen (growth) phase
- **Redensyl:** 150% increase in hair density vs control (4 weeks); advanced peptide complex, RCT-validated
- **Procapil:** Dual-action peptide + DHT blocker, targets follicle miniaturization
- **AnaGain (pea sprout extract):** Prolongs telogen-to-anagen transition, enhances hair cycle length
- **Saw Palmetto (320mg standardized):** 5-alpha reductase inhibitor; mixed RCT efficacy but mechanistically sound for DHT-responsive androgenetic alopecia (6–12 week onset typical)
