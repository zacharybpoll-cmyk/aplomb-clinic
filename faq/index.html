Products

Do I need a prescription for any APLOMB. product?

No. The Serum is a topical cosmetic. Daily, Roots, Calm, and Breath are dietary supplements. None require a prescription.

Is Aplomb. FDA-approved?

No supplement or topical cosmetic is FDA-approved — that is a category that applies to drugs only. Daily, Roots, Calm, and Breath are dietary supplements; their statements have not been evaluated by the FDA. The Serum is regulated as a cosmetic.

Can I take all five products together?

Yes. The line is designed to work together. Daily covers the population-level deficiencies; Roots, Calm, and Breath each target a specific side-effect pattern. The Serum is topical and does not interact with the supplements.

Do you offer a bundle discount?

Subscribers save 10-15 percent on every product. Bundling all five in a single subscription order saves an additional small amount on shipping; the live site shows the running total in your bag.

Side effects + the GLP-1 window

When should I start?

Ideally month 1 or 2 of the GLP-1, before the dose-escalation window for Calm and before the volume-loss window for the Serum. The latest you should start any of them is before month 4. Past month 7, you are working from a deficit that may need a clinician to undo — especially for the face.

Is Aplomb. safe with my GLP-1 medication?

Yes. None of the actives are known to interact pharmacologically with GLP-1 receptor agonists. As with any supplement, discuss with your prescriber if you are pregnant, nursing, or under medical care.

What if I quit my GLP-1?

Daily and Roots stay useful through the recovery window: most people’s lab values and hair follicles take six to twelve months to fully reset. Calm and Breath ramp down naturally as the gastric-stasis side effect resolves.

Will Aplomb. blunt my GLP-1 weight-loss?

No. None of the actives interfere with GLP-1 receptor signaling, satiety, or gastric emptying meaningfully. Calm specifically supports the GI window without changing titration.

Science + sourcing

Where can I see the evidence?

Each product page has a citations section linking to the relevant studies. The full Evidence page lists everything we cite, organized by product. The Biology page expands each mechanism into long-form.

Who manufactures Aplomb.?

The Serum is manufactured in Australia (the cosmetic-peptide formulation specialty market). Daily, Roots, Calm, and Breath are manufactured in GMP-certified US facilities. All four supplements are third-party tested.

How do you decide what goes in each formulation?

The actives in each product are chosen against the strongest published evidence for the specific GLP-1 side effect — not against general-purpose ‘women’s wellness’ criteria. We deliberately leave out ingredients that have no RCT support in the relevant pattern (biotin in Roots is the clearest example).
