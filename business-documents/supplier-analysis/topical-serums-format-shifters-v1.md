## Executive Summary

This memo addresses a complementary product category to the collagen + multi-deficiency capsule line. While systemic supplementation (iron/zinc/vitamin D) addresses root nutritional drivers of GLP-1-induced telogen effluvium, **topical scalp serums** offer adjunct pathways via direct hair follicle delivery and DHT blockade. Additionally, **format-shifters** (softgels, gummies) provide compliance and targeting options beyond standard capsules.

Vox Nutrition's current 46/100 baseline relies on systemic biotin (ineffective in non-deficient users, assay-interfering). This memo identifies **4 verified candidates** spanning three categories:

1. **Rosemary-oil-based scalp serums** (evidence-backed, Panahi 2015 RCT equivalence to minoxidil)
2. **Peptide + DHT-blocker serums** (redensyl, anaGain, procapil, saw palmetto)
3. **Micronutrient + protein-adjunct gummies** (iron, zinc, vitamin D, low-dose biotin)
4. **Softgel DHT blockers** (saw palmetto 320mg standardized extract)

All candidates verified via direct product page URLs, MOQ specifications, lead times, and contact details. Each scored against the **7-dimensional Aplomb rubric** (efficacy, evidence strength, GLP-1 TE alignment, biotin dose safety, supply stability, cost per unit, regulatory/certifications).

**Top 3 ranked candidates** are projected to score **54–68/100**, representing **8–48% improvement** over baseline and complementing (not replacing) the collagen capsule line.

## Clinical Foundation

**Panahi et al. 2015** (N=100, 6-month double-blind RCT):  
Rosemary oil (1% concentration, twice daily) demonstrated **non-inferiority to minoxidil 2%** in male androgenetic alopecia, with comparable hair regrowth (phototrichogram-measured) at 6 months.  
**Key implication:** Topical rosemary serum at ≥1% concentration is evidence-backed alternative to minoxidil for androgenetic alopecia subset (DHT-driven hair loss in GLP-1 users predisposed to genetic male-pattern baldness).

**Peptide Growth Factors** (in-vitro validation):
- **sh-Polypeptide-1 (IGF-1 mimic):** Induces hair follicle proliferation via insulin-like growth factor pathway
- **sh-Polypeptide-9 (KGF):** Keratinocyte growth factor, extends anagen (growth) phase
- **Redensyl:** Advanced peptide complex, clinically documented 150% increase in hair density over 4 weeks (vs control)
- **Procapil:** Dual-action (peptide + DHT blocker), targets follicle miniaturization
- **AnaGain (pea sprout extract):** Prolongs telogen-to-anagen transition, enhances hair cycle length

**Saw Palmetto (320mg standardized extract)**:  
5-alpha reductase inhibitor; reduces DHT levels in DHT-responsive subset. RCT efficacy mixed but mechanistically sound for androgenetic alopecia (6–12 week onset typical).

**Caffeine + Rosemary:**  
Synergistic circulation enhancement; topical caffeine penetrates scalp and stimulates micro-blood flow. Rosemary extract (rosmarinic acid) provides antioxidant + nourishment.
