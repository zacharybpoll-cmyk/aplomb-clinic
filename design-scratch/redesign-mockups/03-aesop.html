Face

GLP-1 facial change. Two drivers, not one.

Volume below, dermal thinning above. The hollowness is fat-pad loss, which a topical cannot reach. The quieter change is dermal: the dermis thins as caloric restriction drops the substrate fibroblasts need to make collagen. That layer is where the Serum works.

Hair

Telogen effluvium follows rapid weight loss like clockwork.

The body reads rapid loss as stress and shifts follicles to rest. The shed begins around month four. Iron, vitamin D, and zinc, dosed for the deficiencies the published data implicates, address what triggered it. Not biotin.

Nausea

Delayed gastric emptying, the vagal nerve on alert.

Forty-four percent report nausea, worst in the titration window, median episode eight days. Ginger at one gram daily and B6 are the adjacent-population evidence base. Most people quit before the dose works.

Breath

Hyposalivation, ketosis, and gastric stasis.

Three converging mechanisms produce volatile sulfur compounds. Sugar-free mints do not address any of the three. Zinc acetate binds them; xylitol and green-tea polyphenols shift the oral microbiome.
