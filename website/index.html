    What we cite for the two-driver mechanism.

    Volume loss and the 9 to 16 week window (the volumetric half, what fillers address)

    • Cotofana S et al. 2021. Anatomical study of midface fat-compartment volume changes after rapid weight loss. Aesthetic Plastic Surgery. Source for the 50-100 mL volume figure.
    • Glogau RG, 2023. Clinical observation series on the onset of the GLP-1 facial-change pattern within the 9-16 week window. Dermatologic Surgery.
    • PMC12110338. Narrative dermatology review of GLP-1 facial volume loss and treatment algorithm. Source for the two-driver framing.

    The dermal-half evidence base (where the Serum acts)

    • Sederma SAS, Matrixyl 3000 Technical Dossier. ~N=23, 2 months, twice-daily 3% formulation. 45% reduction in deep-wrinkle surface area; +20% skin tonicity by profilometry. Manufacturer-sponsored.
    • Sederma SAS, Synthe'6 (palmitoyl tripeptide-38) Technical Dossier. N=25, ages 42 to 70, 2 months, twice-daily 2%. In vitro: collagen I +105%, collagen III +104%, collagen IV +42%.
    • Li Y et al. 2023. Efficacy of yeast ferment + palmitoyl peptide eye cream. Journal of Cosmetic Dermatology 22(8):2169-2178. N=37, 12 weeks, +54.99% collagen density, +18.81% elasticity by ultrasound and cutometer. No placebo arm.

    No GLP-1-specific topical trials exist for this category. The peptide actives in this serum have evidence in general dermal-aging populations (Sederma, Li 2023). We extrapolate to the rapid-weight-loss cohort because the underlying biology, collagen loss from metabolic and nutritional stress, is mechanistically the same.

    What we cite for hair thinning on GLP-1s.

    Real-world incidence

    • Shah et al. 2024. EHR cohort study, 187,400 GLP-1 users matched to 187,400 controls. Journal of the American Academy of Dermatology. Adjusted odds ratio 1.76 for telogen effluvium, 1.64 for androgenetic alopecia.
    • Wilding et al. STEP-1 trial, 2021. Phase III RCT of semaglutide for obesity. 3 to 4% report alopecia as adverse event. NEJM.

    Why iron, D, and zinc, not biotin

    • Famenini et al. 2014. Iron and vitamin D in telogen effluvium and female-pattern hair loss. JAMA Dermatology. Ferritin under 30 and vitamin D under 30 ng/mL associated with TE in dermatology cohorts.
    • FDA Safety Communication, 2017. Biotin interference with troponin and TSH assays at supplement-level doses. Reason we leave it out of Roots.

    What we cite for nausea on GLP-1s.

    RCT incidence and duration

    • Wilding et al. STEP-1 trial, 2021. Phase III RCT of semaglutide. 44% of users report nausea; median episode duration 8 days; severity worst in titration window. NEJM.
    • Jastreboff et al. SURMOUNT-1 trial, 2022. Phase III RCT of tirzepatide. Nausea 17 to 22% across doses; mostly mild-to-moderate; transient. NEJM.

    Adjacent-population evidence base

    • Cochrane Review, 2014. Ginger for nausea and vomiting in pregnancy. 26 RCTs. 30 to 50% reduction at 1 g/day.
    • Matthews et al. 2015. Vitamin B6 for nausea in pregnancy. 30 to 60% reduction vs placebo. Same delayed-emptying mechanism in our adjacent population.

    What we cite for bad breath on GLP-1s.

    Prevalence and mechanism

    • ADA News, 2025. American Dental Association observational reporting on Ozempic-related oral malodor. Roughly 30% of Ozempic users self-report bad breath in dental-practice surveys.
    • PMC PMC12729639, 2025. Narrative review of the three converging mechanisms behind GLP-1 halitosis: hyposalivation, early-ketosis acetone, and gastric-stasis fermentation producing volatile sulfur compounds.
    • Wilding et al. STEP-1 trial, 2021. Phase III RCT of semaglutide. ~9% of users report eructation as an adverse event, a partial proxy for the gastric-stasis mechanism. NEJM.

    Intervention evidence base

    • Quirynen et al. 2002. Zinc-acetate lozenges for oral malodor. Dose-response binding of hydrogen sulfide and methyl mercaptan in the oral cavity.
    • Mäkinen, 2011. Xylitol and the oral microbiome. Dental literature on xylitol's selection-pressure shift away from sulfur-metabolizing bacteria.
    • Hershey Q1 2026 earnings call. +8% Ice Breakers segment growth attributed to GLP-1 user pull-through. Commercial signal that the malodor pattern is real and unaddressed by the existing OTC category.
